Welcome to the NW Steelheaders open seats webpage.

This website is to help boat owners with available seats to find others to fish with. As a passenger sign up for when you’re available and boat owners will reach out. Sign up for the days you’re available but be aware some invites can be short notice!

Please note, priority will be given to NW Steelheaders members. To learn more about joining our organization go here for more information.
